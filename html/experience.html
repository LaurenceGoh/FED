My experience in Singapore Polytechnic so far

So far, I have enjoyed every bit of my life in Singapore Polytechnic. Although it took a while to adapt to a new place, it was my classmates and lecturers that helped my life here much better in secondary school. Every classroom now has air conditioning, which is very nice since I have to walk a long distance from Dover MRT to my class. Classes were interactive as we had to use our personal laptops daily.

My favourite modules

Some of my favourite modules are Fundamentals of Programming (FOP), Front-End web Development (FED) and Mathematics.

FOP is one of my favourite subjects as I like to do programming. JavaScript is the main programming language that is being taught during class and every lesson has been enjoyable. The above image is a program where I had to create a menu and storing properties in an array for my CA2 in the first term.

Math is one of my favourite subjects as I like to deal with numbers. I took both Mathematics and Additional Mathematics back in secondary school and I had performed well in both of them in O levels. However Mathematics for DIT was much different in Secondary school. Although it does not teach some of my favourite math subjects like calculus or trigonometry, I still enjoyed it overall.

FED was interesting as we had to create our own webpages while experimenting at the same time. It was very fun as we get to see each other's creativity and ideas come out.
